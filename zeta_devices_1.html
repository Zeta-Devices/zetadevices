About

ZETA started with a simple question: why do we need a phone to talk to AI?

Most AI tools live inside apps, on phones, behind screens that schools ban and workplaces restrict. That means the people who could benefit most — students, workers, curious minds — often can't access them when they need them most. ZETA was built to change that.

The idea came from a 12 year old in Australia who had already built a Minecraft mod powered by AI, researched electric bike builds, and spent countless hours thinking about how technology could be simpler, smaller, and more useful. The question wasn't whether AI could fit in your pocket — it was why nobody had done it properly yet.

The Rabbit R1 tried. It sold 100,000 units and disappointed most of them. The software was buggy, the hardware was underwhelming, and the company never figured out how to make it genuinely useful. ZETA is the answer to that failure — faster AI, no subscription, no locked ecosystem, just a device that works.

Every ZETA is hand built, quality tested, and shipped only when it's ready. There are no factories, no assembly lines, no corners cut. Just one person who cares about getting it right.

ZETA is powered by Groq — one of the fastest AI inference systems in the world. Responses are near instant. There's no monthly fee. Customers use their own free Groq API key, which means ZETA never holds your data, never charges you to think, and never goes down because a company pulled the plug on a server.

This is just the beginning. A second model is already in planning — more powerful, dual cameras, bigger screen. But the goal has never changed: put genuinely useful AI in your pocket, without asking you to stare at your phone to get it.

FAQ

What is ZETA?
ZETA is a portable AI device — a small handheld gadget that lets you ask questions and get instant AI-powered answers using your voice or the touchscreen. No phone required.
Do I need a subscription?
No. ZETA uses Groq's free API tier, which means you sign up for a free Groq account, enter your key into the device once, and that's it. No monthly fees, no hidden costs.

Every ZETA is hand built and quality tested before it ships. As soon as your device passes testing it goes straight in the post. Join the waitlist to be notified when the next batch is ready.
How much does ZETA cost?
ZETA is $250 AUD. That includes the device, a 3D printed case, and full setup instructions. No extras, no upsells.
What can ZETA actually do?
ZETA can answer questions on almost any topic, help you study, explain concepts, write things, do maths, tell you the weather, and more. Control it by voice or touchscreen — whichever you prefer. It also has Snake built in for when you just want to have fun.
